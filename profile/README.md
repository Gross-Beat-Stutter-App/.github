## 🚀 Introduction
Gross Beat for Windows is an Image-Line effect plugin that captures a rolling buffer of your audio and lets you rewrite how it plays back through two envelopes — time and volume. Placed on a track in FL Studio, it converts everyday loops, vocals, and one-shots into rhythmic repeats, gates, and scratch-style edits without any manual slicing. The whole effect is preset-driven, so Gross Beat is a quick way to find movement for a pattern and audition ideas fast.

## ✨ Highlights

### Sound design
- **Time envelope** — Stretch, rewind, and pitch audio by drawing a curve over the playback timeline.
- **Volume envelope** — Open and close gain to build gates, swells, and rhythmic chops.
- **Scratch and pitch moves** — Combine both envelopes for scratch effects and dramatic pitch bends.

### Workflow
- **36 preset slots** — Recall a complete stutter or gate instantly and grow your own library.
- **Tempo sync** — Snap every point to the FL Studio grid so effects stay locked to your beat.
- **Live automation** — Automate the active slot so the movement changes across your arrangement.

## 📥 How to Install
1. Click the **Download** button above to get the latest version of Gross Beat.
2. Run the downloaded installer and follow the on-screen setup steps.
3. Start FL Studio, rescan your plugins if needed, and load Gross Beat on a channel or mixer track.

## 🎯 Getting Up and Running
1. Add Gross Beat as an effect on the track that carries the audio you want to reshape.
2. Play the pattern so sound runs through the plugin.
3. Choose a time preset or a volume preset from the slots along the top edge.
4. Listen as the effect applies to your pattern, then adjust the curve points to taste.
5. Automate the selected preset slot so the effect develops through your song.

## ❓ Common Questions
**Is Gross Beat free?**
Gross Beat is bundled with certain FL Studio editions and is also sold as a paid add-on. To try it without buying, use FL Studio's trial, which lets you explore the plugin first.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
It works on current 64-bit Windows releases such as Windows 10 and Windows 11 inside a compatible FL Studio version.

**Can I add free Gross Beat presets?**
Yes. Alongside the built-in presets, the community shares many free Gross Beat presets you can load into the open slots to expand what the plugin can do.
